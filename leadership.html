Andrew MacKay – Founder
Andrew, a former Senior Management Consultant with Maritime Management Consulting and ECS-Federal, founded DCT to serve the growing Risk Management needs of the Maritime and Offshore Oil Industry and tackle the inevitable uncertainty associated with maritime and offshore operations at all levels. Mr. MacKay is a maritime professional with broad experience in Risk Management and Cost Avoidance including Program Management, Production Management and Operations Management for both commercial and government programs that exceed a total value of $8 billion.

Mr. MacKay received a Bachelor’s of Science degree in Marine Systems Engineering from the United States Merchant Marine Academy and a Masters of Business Administration from the University of Florida, School of Business Administration. He is a licensed Merchant Marine Engineer of unlimited horsepower. Furthermore, Andrew has certificates from the Kepner-Tragoe Project Management Group and endorsements in contract law from the University of Miami along with a Contract Claims and Litigation Avoidance Certification from the Condor Group. Andrew is a certified Risk Management Professional with the Project Management Institute.
