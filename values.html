Our Values The values of DCT serve as the groundwork and foundation of our company. They are fundamental to our business model and serve as a roadmap for our future.

Our company values are to:
- Add meaning and value while being a good steward of our client’s financial resources
- Create work that matters and makes a difference
- Go the extra mile
- Seek open, honest and clear communication
- Support members who innovate and strive for excellence
- Deliver the best solution for every client as cost efficiently as possible
- Be adventurous and embrace change
- Remain humble and grateful in our success
- Be friendly and have a sense of humor

Although profit is important to any organization, it does not supersede DCT’s aim for natural outgrowth and successful long-term business performance.
