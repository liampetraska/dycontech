More than any other industry, the complex environment in which companies in the Maritime and Offshore Oil Industry operate subjects them to increased costs and liabilities from environmental and workplace accidents. Exposure to accidents and environmental risks goes beyond the financial impact and can affect the company’s reputation and right to remain in business.

At DCT, we draw on our experience, available resources and modern management practices to work to prevent environmental and workplace accidents from occurring.

To better protect our clients from Environmental Risk and Accident Prevention, DCT offers a variety of client focused services:
- Due Diligence Investigations
- Incident and Claim Valuations
- Site Condition Uncertainties
- Incident Prevention Analysis
- Product and Service Risk Assessment
- Environmental Risk Modeling
- Crisis Management
