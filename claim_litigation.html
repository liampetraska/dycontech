Any litigation, no matter how small, can be costly and time consuming. Depending on a company’s line of business, litigation can also affect a company’s reputation and weaken future business opportunities. Even where no potential problems loom on the horizon, organizations can still actively reduce their risk of being drawn into a claim or litigation. Foremost among these strategies is through the effective development and implementation of a Risk Management and Cost Avoidance program.

How DCT helps to protect our clients:
- Claim Defense Support
- Project Documentation
- Claim Analysis
- Integrated Change Control and Valuation
- Program Auditing
- Contract Assessment and Specification Review
