Dynamic Consulting Technologies (DCT) is a firm dedicated to providing Risk Management services for the Maritime and Offshore Oil Industry. Our experienced consultants have worked in shipyards, ports and aboard vessels operating internationally and throughout the Gulf of Mexico. DCT uses Risk Management to better aid our client’s decision making, thereby enabling them to meet their business objectives. As a firm, we have seen time and time again where projects and companies fail to meet performance goals solely due to a failure to implement a successful Risk Management Program.

Our firm serves clients, both large and small. Below are selected highlights from our experience.

- Due Diligence investigation and forensic cost accounting for a claim that resulted from catastrophic damage caused by Hurricane Katrina—claim exceeded $1 billion

- Successful implementation of cost avoidance plan for profitable new-build project for domestic shipbuilding company—contract exceeding $500 million

- Performed assignments for international law firm to conduct a review of the global shipbuilding industry as it related to U.S. domestic demand in response to a firm facing a $300 million liability

- Partnered with a Government Agency faced with uncontrolled cost growth and contract appropriations exceeding $50 million to implement an effective Risk Mitigation plan

- Worked with a Mississippi based maritime equipment manufacturing and refurbishing company developing a sustainable and Risk Management based business development plan
