Risks in Project and Operations Management can be predictable or completely unanticipated. The benefits of implementing an effective Risk Management plan mean fewer setbacks and costly delays. This translates into better financial performance and increased future opportunities. DCT provides comprehensive Risk Management services that are effective and palatable and allow your company to deliver products on time, on budget and with the quality standards your customers expect.

Our expertise includes key components needed for successful project execution:
- Proposal and Contract Reviews
- Contractual Risk Management
- Project Cost and Schedule Analysis
- Earned Value Management
- Workplace Safety Audits
- Risk Exposure Analysis
- Operational Risk Assessment
- Risk Simulations and Statistical Modeling
